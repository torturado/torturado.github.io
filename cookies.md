# Cookie and Storage Policy

Canonical URL: https://torturado.github.io/cookies

## Overview

- The policy says the site does not use its own tracking cookies.
- Essential browser storage is used for preferences and calculator behavior.

## Local storage

- Saving calculator preferences.
- Remembering theme settings.
- Keeping calculator state available during use.

## Third-party services

- GitHub Pages may use essential platform-level cookies or logging for security.
- Umami Analytics is described as cookie-free.

## Privacy controls

- Users can clear local storage through browser settings.
- Private or incognito browsing avoids persistent local storage.
- The policy says no cookie opt-out flow is required because the site does not use tracking cookies directly.

## Browser management examples

- Chrome: clear browsing data from Privacy and Security settings.
- Firefox: manage Cookies and Site Data from Privacy & Security.
- Safari: manage website data from Privacy preferences.
- Edge: clear browsing data from Privacy, search, and services.
