# Privacy Policy

Canonical URL: https://torturado.github.io/privacy

## Information collected

- Calculator math runs in the browser.
- Preferences such as theme settings may be stored locally on the device.
- The policy says no personal information is collected, stored, or processed by the site itself.

## How data is used

- To perform calculator functions locally.
- To remember local preferences.
- To measure anonymous usage through Umami Analytics.

## Cookies and local storage

- The site says it does not use cookies for its own analytics.
- Local storage is used for essential preferences and calculator behavior.

## Third-party services

- Hosting: GitHub Pages.
- Analytics: Umami Analytics, described as privacy-focused and cookie-free.

## Security and user controls

- Calculator processing stays local in the browser.
- The site is served over HTTPS.
- Users can clear local storage in their browser settings.
- The policy says Do Not Track signals are respected.
